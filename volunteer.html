Volunteering Experiences

I have participated in a lot of volunteering events.

Hmong Wisconsin Association 2015 - 2018

I had the opportunity to part of the setup team and backstage crew at the Madison Hmong New Year. I was in charge of making sure the participating groups were ready to be cued onto stage. I also had the chance to be part of the sound booth, as an assistant.


Southeast Asia Dance Organization-University of Wisconsin-Madison 2015 - 2018

I was given the opportunity to volunteer my time as a dancer to practice and perform with the organization. We had the chance to be the opening act for the Madison Hmong New Year of 2018.


Friendship Club 2017 - 2018

During my last two years in High School, I joined the Friendship club. We travelled aroudn the neighborhood to help the community have clean yards. Our main goal was to keep the environment clean from populltion.


Vocal Essence 2017 - 2018

I was part of a new choir group created for students in the Metro area of Minnesota. We sung at Ausberg College and at the Minnesota Orchestra Hall. Our group was called Singers of This Age youth choir (SOTA).
